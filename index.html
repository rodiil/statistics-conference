About

About the Conference

The Statistics Research Conference brings together researchers, faculty members, students, and practitioners to share studies, methodologies, applications, and innovations in statistics and data science.

The conference aims to promote research collaboration, strengthen statistical practice, and encourage the use of evidence in addressing relevant academic, scientific, and community concerns.
